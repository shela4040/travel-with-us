Paris, the enchanting capital of France, is a city that epitomizes romance, elegance, and history. Known as "The City of Light," Paris boasts a stunning array of architectural marvels, including the iconic Eiffel Tower, the majestic Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the opulent Palace of Versailles. The city's rich cultural heritage is on display at world-renowned museums such as the Louvre, home to the Mona Lisa, and the Musée d'Orsay, which houses an extensive collection of Impressionist masterpieces. Strolling along the Seine River, visitors can enjoy picturesque views of historic bridges and charming riverside cafes. The cobblestone streets of Montmartre, with its artistic legacy and the stunning Sacré-Cœur Basilica, offer a glimpse into the bohemian spirit of Paris
